    Features

    Simple and Easy

    Experience OpenBosniana OS without the need for installation. Just create a bootable USB flash drive and you're ready to go. Itâ€™s designed to work out of the box and comes preloaded with all the essential applications for everyday use.

    Privacy

    OpenBosniana OS ensures your data is handled safely and reliably. Free from trackers and telemetry features, it offers robust privacy tools including AnonSurf, Tor, a custom Firefox browser, cryptographic tools, and more.

    Safety

    OpenBosniana OS is built with security in mind. Enjoy native support for full disk encryption, rapid security updates, and a fortified Debian kernel to safeguard your sensitive data.

    Developer-Friendly

    OpenBosniana OS is tailored for developers, integrating popular programming languages, compilers, interpreters, libraries, and development frameworks. Whether pre-installed or just a command away in our software repository, it streamlines and accelerates your development process.

    Productivity

    Boost your productivity with LibreOffice's comprehensive office suite. Create documents, presentations, drawings, spreadsheets, and databases with ease. Seamlessly import and export PDF and Microsoft Office documents for maximum compatibility.

    Multimedia / Design / Gaming

    Create stunning 3D art with Blender, edit photos in GIMP, and design vector graphics with Inkscape. Plus, access over 7,000 games with Steam. And there's so much more to explore!
